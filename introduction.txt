Brief Introduction About Myself
I am ThankGod Ibeh, a Media Technical Director, Digital Entrepreneur, and passionate Youth Advocate from Lagos, Nigeria. My professional journey is driven by a deep commitment to bridging the education-opportunity gap for African youth, empowering them with essential digital skills and guiding them towards fulfilling career paths.

As the Co-Founder and Digital Talent Director of StarLab Academy, I lead programs that integrate digital literacy, mentorship, and career guidance into underserved communities. My expertise spans media production, technical direction, and program development, allowing me to merge technical education with impactful storytelling to inspire and equip young individuals for the future.
